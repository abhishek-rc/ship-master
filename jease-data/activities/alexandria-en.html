Landmarks of Alexandria

Landmarks of Alexandria

Duration 4 Hours
Medium Level

Discover the historical wonders of Alexandria’s past on a journey that unveils its most iconic landmarks. Begin with a visit to the hauntingly atmospheric Catacombs of Shawqafa, a subterranean maze blending Egyptian, Greek, and Roman styles. A short photo stop to marvel at the towering Qait Bay, built atop the ruins of the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Continue your journey for a photo stop by Abu El Abbas Mosque, an architectural gem of Islamic heritage. Conclude your exploration with a guided tour to the Bibliotheca Alexandrina.

Includes the following
  • Transportation to the Catacombs of Shawqafa
  • Guided Tour of the Catacombs
  • Photo stops at Qait Bay and Abu El Abbas Mosque
  • Entry and guided tour of the Bibliotheca Alexandrina
  • Drive back to port

Bibliotheca Alexandrina and Montaza Gardens

Bibliotheca Alexandrina and Montaza Gardens

Duration 4.5 Hours
Medium Level

Embrace the elegance of Alexandria with a day dedicated to its most beautiful and serene attractions. Drive through Mansheya square where the equestrian statue of Mohamed Ali, the founder of modern Egypt greets the visitors to the city and view the Marble Monument dedicated to the Unknown Soldier. As you begin your trip with a visit to the Bibliotheca Alexandrina, a stunning tribute to the ancient world’s greatest library. Explore its impressive reading halls, exhibitions, and the Manuscripts &Antiquities Museum. Then, escape into the lush Montaza Gardens, once the royal retreat of Egypt’s last kings. Surrounded by swaying palms, fountains, and sea views, stroll the grounds of the Montaza Palace, and soak in its regal charm.

Includes the following
  • Transportation to the Bibliotheca Alexandrina
  • Scenic drive along Mansheya Square
  • Guided tour of Bibliotheca Alexandrina
  • Free time to explore and take photos in Montaza Gardens and view of Palace Grounds
  • Drive back to the port

Greco-Roman Alexandria

Duration 5 Hours
Medium Level

Embark on a leisurely journey through the streets of Alexandria, beginning with a photo stop by the Citadel of Qait Bey, a 15th-century fortress built on the ruins of the legendary Lighthouse of Alexandria. Also a photo stop to Marvel the architectural beauty of the Abu El Abbas Mosque before heading to the Catacombs of Shawqafa &the Pompeii’s Pillar/ Serapeum of Alexandria. Conclude your tour with a visit of the Roman Amphitheater in Kom El Dekka before driving to the library to explore and finally enjoy a scenic drive along the famous Bibliotheca Alexandrina before reaching the port.

Panoramic Alexandria

Panoramic Alexandria

Duration 5 Hours
Easy Level

Embark on a captivating journey through Alexandria, begin your exploration at Mansheya Square, home to the impressive equestrian statue of Mohamed Ali Pasha, the founder of modern Egypt and witness the memorial site of the Unknown Soldier. Continue your journey for a photo stop by the Mosque of Abu al-Abbas al-Mursi, Alexandria 's largest mosque and a photo stop by the Citadel of Qait Bay where the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria stands. After a stop at a local venue, continue to the Montaza Palace Gardens, where the royal summer residence of King Farouk resides. Conclude your tour with a photo stop at the Bibliotheca Alexandrina, before returning to port.

Includes the following
  • Panoramic drive through Alexandria
  • Drive to Mansheya Square and the memorial site of the Unknown Soldier
  • Photo stop by the Mosque of Abu al-Abbas and Citadel of Qait Bay
